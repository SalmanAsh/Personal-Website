About me

My name is Salman. I am 20 years old. I was born in Mandi Bahauddin, Pakistan, but I am currently living in Manchester with my parents and a sister. I moved to Italy at the age of 5 and attended my primary and secondary at Rodari school. I completed my GCSEs and levels 3 Engineering diploma in the UK. I am currently studying Computer Science BSc at The University of Manchester. My primary interests are Mathematics, Web-development, and cosmology/astronomy. I have experience creating websites using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. I have also been part of web-based projects with my team members at the University (check my Portfolio for more).


Why CS?

I coded at the age of 15 for the first time. I programmed a tiny piece of hardware made of LEDs, called Microbit. I used MakeCode software to input a message based on some letters and sounds. I also programmed a line-follower car using Arduino. Ever since then I have been involved with computers and coding. I decided Computer Science to be career path after I finished my GCSEs.
